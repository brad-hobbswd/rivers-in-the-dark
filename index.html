About This Story

Brad thought life had become routine. Work, responsibilities, and unanswered questions weighed heavily on his heart. Through unexpected moments with his son Gabe, simple acts of compassion, and the gentle leading of Jesus, a journey begins that changes everything. Life begins changing through quiet whispers, unexpected moments, and rivers that flow through places many never notice.
