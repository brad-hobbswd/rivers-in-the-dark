About The Author

Stories often begin in quiet places. Rivers in the Dark was born from faith, reflection, fatherhood, and moments that reveal purpose in unexpected ways.

Bradley Hobbs

Bradley Hobbs writes with a heart for faith, healing, hope, and everyday moments that reveal deeper truths. Through storytelling and devotion centered writing, his desire remains simple. Encourage hearts, strengthen faith, and remind readers that Jesus still reaches people in quiet places.

Rivers in the Dark follows themes of purpose, fatherhood, spiritual growth, and learning to trust God's direction through ordinary moments.

"Some rivers begin where sunlight reaches. Others are born where nobody sees."

Grace often flows through unseen places first.

Behind The Story

Brad's journey reflects questions many people carry. Questions about purpose, pressure, fear, family, and faith. Through simple moments with his son Gabe and the gentle leading of Jesus, small changes begin creating larger transformations.

The story reminds readers that powerful movements of God often begin quietly. A conversation. A prayer. A choice. A moment of compassion. Rivers rarely begin with noise.
